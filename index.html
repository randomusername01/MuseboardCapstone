Tutorial

  • Text: Click the Text button to add a text box.
  • Media: Click the Media button to add images or GIFs.
  • Link: Click the Link button to add a clickable link.
  • Draw: Click the Draw button to enter drawing mode. Double-click it to open drawing options where you can customize color, thickness, and tools.
  • Clear / Undo: Use the Clear button to click and delete items on the canvas and the Undo button to revert your last action.
  • Settings & Theme Customization: Click the Settings button to open the settings dropdown. Here you can customize themes and other preferences.
